Complaint Reporting Procedure

Passengers who suspect a driver is under the influence of drugs or alcohol may report the incident by:

In-Ride Help (recommended)

If you need help now, press the button below. This will alert Park City Rides, trigger a safety review, and pause ride dispatching to the driver.
In an emergency, call 911 first.

  • Email: support@parkcityrides.com
  • Call the Park City Rides support line: (917) 575-1955

Park City Rides will review all complaints, investigate as needed, and remove drivers from service if found in violation. Where appropriate, violations will be reported to law enforcement.
