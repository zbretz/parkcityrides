How fares are determined

Inputs

  • Passenger enters pickup, drop‑off, and time in the app.
  • The app uses the provided locations to determine trip mileage.

Base Calculation

Fare = Per‑mile rate × Distance (minimum fare applies)

Adjustments (may apply)

  • Time of day (e.g., late‑night surcharge).
  • Ride type / vehicle tier (e.g., XL for larger groups).

Price Transparency

Passenger sees the calculated fare before requesting the ride.
