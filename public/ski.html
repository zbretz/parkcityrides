FAQ

Where do lessons take place?

Choose your preferred resort (e.g., Park City Mountain / Canyons). Your instructor will confirm a convenient meeting point.

Do you provide lift tickets or gear?

Lift tickets and equipment are not included. We can recommend rental partners and meet you at the base.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Cancellations up to 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Within 24 hours, a partial refund may apply depending on circumstances.

Can I book multiple days?

Yes—many families book 2–3 days to progress quickly. You can add multiple sessions in the booking flow.
